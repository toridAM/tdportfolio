case study

This was a fictional case study performed for apartment buildings. One key for security entrance and one key for renters unit.

Two keys are required from renters to give access to their security building entrance and unit.

Problem

  1. Non-renters has key to apartment but not to security entrance
  2. Renter unavailable by being out of the building, hired services need access to security building an renters unit.
  3. Renter has health issues and is unable to respond to call or text.
  4. On Weekends management is unavailable to let non-renters into building/unit

Research

The research took place at a apartment complex.

The goal was to ask 100 people.

Selected question

  1. Would you rather have 2 keys or use an app to enter the building/unit?
  2. How do you feel about giving your username and password to people that you will need to enter your apartment building and unit?
  3. do you feel your privacy is being protected

findings

80 out of 100 people were interested in using an app vs using a key system.

  • Building has a call system in place but does not work 80% of the time.
  • Renters where not able to recieve message or call with the system in place.

challenge

  • Renters where not at home.
  • Renters not able to receive call and connect with non-renters.
  • Renters where unresponsive due to health issues.

conclustion

Non-renters where unable to enter the security building and renters unit even when they where expected because the call system was not connecting non-renter and renter together.

The key app allows renters to enter their security building and not need keys only their phone.

The Key app allows non-renters to enter the security building and renters unit when renter has health issues and is non-responsive

The key app allows non-renters to enter the security building and renters unit when renter is not at home.

The key app allows non-renters to enter the security building and renters unit on weekends when management is off.

the outcome

  • The key app eliminates the use of keys, but will need app downloaded on your phone.
  • The key app makes it easy to enter a security building and renters unit when required.
