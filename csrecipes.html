introduction

This was a fictional case study performed on the company Allreceipes. This website was created to give the user a platform to create a variety of dishes, so they could save money by not going out to eat more than 2 times per week.

my role

my role was to find solutions to add new dishes to a user and give them choices of how to prepare a recipe and have them come back to the website to make more creative meals.

problems

The problem was finding a variety of receipes that where easy to make and cost effecient.

goals

the goals where to find out what was important to the user? does the images of the meal matter for the user to prepare a meal? does the user read the reviews from favorite to least? is it improtant for the user to purchasing the ingredients online?

impact

60% of them prefered opions. 100% of them wanted writen directions. 30% of them wanted a video for step by step directions. 100% of them would click on an image if it was enticing. 30% of them wanted to beable to purchase the ingredients on the site.

early ideation

i gathered inspiration from the pictures of dishes that i like, with having choices of how to follow the directions of making a recipe.

testing

People did not recognize the company name. People wanted to have a sample of some dishes. Many people where not interested in taking the survey. People wanted to have a discount if they used the site.

final design

The final design was based on the images, reviews and choices of how to prepare the recipes.

development

I built the responsive landing page on github using visual studio code. I design the prototype in figma. at this time this app is in the infinte stage and needs to be created by a front end developer so that it is able to be launched into action.
