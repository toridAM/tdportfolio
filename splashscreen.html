case study

This was a fictional case study performed for apartment buildings. 2 keys are needed 1 to enter security entrance and 1 to enter renter"s unit.

Problem

Renter's stepped out when expecting visitors.

Renters where unresponsive due to health issues.

Non-renters had 1 key to apartment but not to security entrance

Renters had to pay for extra keys to give out for access to building and unit.

No management available on weekends to let non-renters into building/unit

Research

The goal was to ask 100 people.

The research took place at an apartment complex.

Selected question

Do you feel your privacy is being protected?

Would you rather have 2 keys or use an app to enter the building/unit?

Are you comfortable giving username and password for access to building/unit?

findings

Building has a call system in place but does not work 80% of the time.

80 out of 100 people were interested in using an app vs using a key system.

Renters where not able to recieve message or call with the system in place.

challenge

Best times to talk to Renters.

Elderly renters where not comfortable using an app vs keys.

conclustion

The key app allows renters to enter their security building and not need keys only their phone.

The key app allows non-renters to enter the security building and renters unit when renter is not at home.

The key app allows non-renters to enter the security building and renters unit on weekends when management is off.

The Key app allows non-renters to enter the security building and renters unit when renter has health issues and is non-responsive

2 keys are needed 1 to enter security entrance and 1 to enter renter"s unit, which cost money to make 2 keys for every non-renter you wanted to give access to your appartment/unit.

the outcome

The app eliminates the use of keys

Renter does not need to buy extra keys

non-renters can access the building with the app downloaded on their phone.

To enter the apartment building and unit you will need a phone to down load the app.

The key app makes it easy to enter a security building and renters unit when required.
