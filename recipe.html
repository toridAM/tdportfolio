case study

This was a fictional case study performed on the company Allreceipes.

This website was created to give the user a platform to create a variety of dishes, so they could save money by not going out to eat.

Problem

The problem was that many people did not have a list of receipes that they new how to make.

Research

The research took place at a local groshery store

The goal was to ask 50 out of 100 people 3 questions

Selected question

Would you be interested in cooking a meal if?

  1. You could purchase the ingredients online and have them shipped to you?
  2. The images of the meals looked enticing?

findings

The user was interested in these categories

  • 60% of them prefered opions
  • 30% of them wanted a video for step by step directions
  • 100% of them wanted writen directions
  • 30% of them wanted to beable to purchase the ingredients on the site
  • 100% of them would click on an image if it was enticing

challenge

  • Many people where not interested in taking the survey.
  • People wanted to have a sample of some dishes
  • People wanted to have a discount if they used the site
  • People did not recognize the company name

conclustion

The user did not know what to make but if they made a dish and liked it they would return to the website to make another dish

the outcome

  • Increase in people eating at home cost less money
  • Decrease eating out cost more money
